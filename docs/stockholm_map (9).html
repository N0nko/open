Packing Checklist

  • Winter Coat (insulated, waterproof)
  • Thermal Layers (tops & leggings)
  • Mid-Layers (sweaters/fleece)
  • Insulated Boots w/ good traction
  • Warm Hat, Scarf, Gloves
  • Wool Socks (multiple pairs)
  • Umbrella / Hood (snow or rain)
  • Sunglasses (low winter sun glare)
  • Lip Balm & Moisturizer (dry air)
  • Passport/ID, Cards, Some Cash
  • Power Adapter (Type C/F)
  • Phone & Charger
  • Any Medications + Basic First Aid
  • Hand/Foot Warmers (optional)

Tickets / Reservations

  • Vasa Museum
